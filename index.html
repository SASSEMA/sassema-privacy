Privacy Policy

SASSEMA Study Onboarding

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our research team collects, uses, and protects information gathered through our iOS application ("the App"). Please note that the App is used solely to support participant enrollment in a research study. It does not collect data directly from your smartphone. Those data are collected only by the separate smartphone sensing app that participants may choose to install after providing consent.

1. Purpose of the App

The App is designed to assist participants with the initial steps of joining a research study. Specifically, the App allows participants to:

  • Review and complete an electronic consent form
  • Receive instructions for installing the separate smartphone sensing data collection app
  • Complete questionnaires as part of study enrollment

The App does not collect passive sensing data from your phone.

2. Information We Collect Through This App

2.1 Consent Form

Participants complete an electronic consent form within the App. This form records:

  • Confirmation that the participant has reviewed the study information and is 18 years of age or older
  • Consent to participate in the research study
  • Authorization for the study team to collect data through other tools (e.g., EMA app, sensing app)

2.2 Participant ID

You will be asked to enter a randomly generated participant ID assigned to you by the research team.

2.3 Baseline Questionnaire Responses

The App collects responses to a baseline survey that includes items from questionnaires measuring thoughts, feelings, and behaviors including symptoms of mental health diagnoses. These may include:

  • Symptoms of Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Sleep habits and sleep quality
  • Personality traits
  • Depression symptoms
  • Stress and anxiety symptoms
  • Alcohol use
  • Nicotine use
  • Procrastination
  • Current medications
  • Current and prior mental health diagnoses

These responses help us to describe our participants at the start of the study and measure any changes over time.

3. How We Use the Data Collected in This App

Data collected through this App are used solely for research purposes, including:

  • Documenting participant consent
  • Recording baseline questionnaire responses
  • Supporting study enrollment and setup

No data are used for commercial purposes or advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take multiple steps to protect participant information:

  • Data are encrypted on the phone before HTTPS transmission to our study's secure server (maintained by the university)
  • Identifiable information (e.g., email addresses and contact information) is stored separately from your survey data and linked via your participant ID number
  • Access is restricted to authorized research personnel
  • Data are stored on secure, institution-approved servers

We comply with all applicable institutional, state, and federal regulations governing human subjects research.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share participant data with third parties for commercial use.

Personal identifiers will be accessible only by the study principal investigators for communicating with the participants and compensation purposes.

De-identified (anonymous) data may be shared:

  • With the research team and authorized collaborators
  • With institutional review boards (IRBs) for compliance
  • In de-identified or aggregated form for scientific publications, transparency, and compliance with open science practices

No personally identifiable information will appear in publications or shared datasets.

6. Participant Rights

Participants may:

  • Withdraw from the study at any time
  • Request deletion of their identifiable data (unless already de-identified)
  • Decline to install the smartphone sensing app
  • Decline specific permissions in the sensing app
  • Ask questions about the study or data handling

Participation is voluntary and can be discontinued without penalty.

7. Data Retention

De-identified (anonymous) data collected through this App are retained only as long as necessary to meet the research objectives and comply with institutional policies. De-identified data may be retained for long-term scientific use. All the personal identifiers associated with the collected data will be deleted within one year of the study completion date.
